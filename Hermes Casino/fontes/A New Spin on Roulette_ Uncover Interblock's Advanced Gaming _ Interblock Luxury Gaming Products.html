Interblock Roulette is more than a gameit's a fusion of tradition and innovation.

In Standard Roulette, there are two betting areas: inside and outside. Inside, players place straight-up bets on a number for a 35:1 payout. They can also choose split bets across two numbers for a 17:1 payout. The street bet covers a row of three numbers with an 11:1 payout. Corner bets cover four numbers, offering an 8:1 payout. The line bet covers two rows or six numbers for a 5:1 payout.

Outside bets provide broader options. Players can bet on columns of 12 numbers or select dozenseither the first, second, or third set of 12 numbers, both giving a 2:1 payout. Players can also wager on the number's color, parity, or range. These bets provide a 1:1 payout.
